Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Talk does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Your privacy is built into the architecture of our app.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Talk iOS application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this document.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Talk is designed with privacy by default. We do not collect:

  • personal information (name, email, phone number);
  • location, contacts, or files from your device;
  • usage analytics or behavioral data;
  • any data via background requests — there are none.

All progress and settings are stored locally on your device only.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

Talk offers optional monthly and annual subscriptions that unlock premium features. All payment transactions are processed exclusively by Apple App Store in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to your payment information and do not store any financial data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate advertising networks, analytics SDKs, trackers, or any other third-party data-collection services.

5. Global Privacy Laws (GDPR, CCPA)

Because Talk collects no personal data, the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Brazil's LGPD, and similar laws are met by design. All rights granted to you under those frameworks are inherently protected: there is no data to access, delete, or transfer.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users of any age. We comply with COPPA and equivalent international regulations by default.

7. Security

The absence of any network data transmission eliminates the risk of data breaches. Local data is protected by the standard iOS security sandbox.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you via an App Store update and/or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top always reflects the current version.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please reach us through the App: Settings → Feedback.
