Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Talko – Daily Questions → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period; no partial refunds are issued for unused time.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open Talko → Settings tab → Get Premium → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Why don't I see the free trial?

Introductory offers are only available to accounts that haven't previously subscribed to that plan. If you've used a trial before, the option won't appear again for the same Apple ID.

How do I change the app language?

Open Talko → Settings tab → Language, and choose between English and Ukrainian. The daily question and all category packs update automatically.

The widget isn't updating. What should I do?

Long-press the widget on your Home Screen and check it's still linked to Talko. If the question still looks outdated, try removing and re-adding the widget, or reopen the app once to refresh the data.

I found a bug or have a feature suggestion.

We'd love to hear from you — email us at voloshanov.developer@icloud.com with as much detail as you can (device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce, if it's a bug).
